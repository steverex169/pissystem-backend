Wager Alerts - Tutorial

Wager alerts are now available on two different apps. These apps are available for IOS and Android.

Below are basic instructions and then detailed instructions for different apps.

  • 1. Call customer service to add your USER to the alert server.
  • 2. Download one of the apps on your phone.
  • 3. Allow the app to receive notifications.
  • 4. Make sure the app is allowed to run in the background.

The first app is Catapush.

With this app you ID is actually 1 + your phone number. Please call customer service to add your phone number to the alert server.

The second app is ImPushMe.

After installing this app please create a new channel. Then copy the channel link and send it to customer service so they can add it to the alert server. It should look similar to this. https://impushme.com/c/bUzLV9LwI

Once you have either app installed and customer service has added your phone number or link to the alert server, you can start receiving wager alerts. Go to any customer’s general tab and click on the green icon below.

Enter your user and password then click save.

This will allow your to receive wager alerts from this customer.

Click on Test Alert to make sure it is working.

You should receive the test alert.

Please call Customer Service if you have any problems.
